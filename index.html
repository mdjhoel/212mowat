What's special

Classic maritime cedar shake home on a quiet side street in Saint Andrews-by-the-sea, New Brunswick. This spacious house has lots of room for your family and friends. New and modern with 4 bedrooms, an office, dining room, fully equipped kitchen, a backyard with a large deck with a dining area, BBQ as well as a fire pit for smores with the kids after dinner. There is also a fenced side yard with another deck for lounging surrounded by your garden. The house has plenty of parking (4+) and is walking distance to restaurants, festivals, farmer's market, whale watching and deep sea fishing tours, Kingsbrae Gardens, Minister's island, Katy's Cove salt water swimming beach, our historic main street, and our beautiful waterfront. Please visit the town website for a full list amenities and events.
